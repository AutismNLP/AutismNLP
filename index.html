SUMMARY

Hi! I am a postgraduate student (expected to graduate in 2021) at Harbin Institute of Technology. I am now advised by Prof. Ting Liu in the Research Center for Social Computing and Information Retrieval, Social Prediction group.

I am now working on Event Logic Graph. I am focusing on representation learning for external data (e.g. ontology, knowledge base) in pipeline modules or end-to-end systems. My vision for future research is to bridge this gap and develop agents that everyone can effectively communicate with. I am also interested in representation learning with inductive biases from linguistics.
